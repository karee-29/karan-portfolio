About

The eye
behind the
lens.

The eye behind the lens is guided by a constant pursuit of light, emotion, and storytelling. What began as curiosity has grown into a refined perspective shaped through concerts, street narratives, wildlife encounters, and cinematic visuals. The focus remains on capturing moments that pass in seconds yet hold lasting impact.

From powerful stage performances to quiet in-between expressions, artists like Javed Ali, Shalmali Kholgade, and Naalayak have been documented in their most authentic energy, turning live experiences into enduring visual frames.

Photography here is not just about images, but about preserving atmosphere, emotion, and story with intention and clarity.

— Karan S
